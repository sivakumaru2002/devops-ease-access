## Common Questions

### Q: What happens after I register?
**A:** Your account is created but remains inactive until an admin approves it.

### Q: Can I login before approval?
**A:** You can try, but you'll see a message: "â³ Your account is waiting for admin approval."

### Q: How long does approval take?
**A:** It depends on your admin's availability. Contact your admin if it's urgent.

### Q: What if I forget my password?
**A:** Currently, you'll need to contact an admin. (Password reset feature coming soon!)

### Q: Can I change my username after registration?
**A:** Not currently. Choose carefully!
